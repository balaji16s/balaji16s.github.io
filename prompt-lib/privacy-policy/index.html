App Prompt Lib

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

TL;DR — Prompt Lib works fully offline. There is no account, no server, and no tracking. Nothing you do in the App ever leaves your device.

1. Overview

Prompt Lib ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is a curated, offline library of prompts. You can browse and search the built-in catalog and save your favorites — all without an account and without an internet connection. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. In short: it does not collect or transmit any personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Prompt Lib does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The following is created and stored only on your device:

A. On-Device App Data

  • Your favorites / bookmarks and recently viewed prompts
  • Your in-app settings and preferences
  • A randomly generated local identifier used only as an internal key on your device — it is never transmitted to us or any third party and is not used for tracking

B. Bundled Content

The prompt catalog ships inside the App. Browsing or searching it happens entirely on your device and does not contact any server.

C. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No account — there is no sign-up or login
  • No location data
  • No usage, analytics, or browsing data sent to any server
  • No crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking technologies

3. App Permissions

Prompt Lib requests no sensitive runtime permissions. The App can open an external link (for example, this Privacy Policy or a referenced website) in your device's browser — when you follow such a link, the destination site's own privacy policy applies.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, authentication, or backend services. Prompt Lib is distributed through the Google Play Store, which collects download and device information under its own privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

Your favorites, settings, and the local identifier remain on your device until you clear them within the App or uninstall it. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

6. Data Security

Because no data leaves your device, there is no transmission to secure. On-device data is protected by your device's standard application sandboxing. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or hold any of your personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on our side. You control all on-device data and can erase it at any time within the App or by uninstalling. Where laws such as the GDPR or CPRA apply, these rights are satisfied directly through this on-device control. Questions? Contact madebybala@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Prompt Lib is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be effective when posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
