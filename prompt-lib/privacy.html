Privacy Policy

Prompt Lib — Last Updated: May 15, 2026

Overview

Prompt Lib ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

A. Anonymous Device Identifier

  • We generate a unique, anonymous device ID on your device.
  • This ID is not linked to your personal identity.
  • It's used to track your interactions (votes, copies, submissions) and prevent abuse.
  • Stored locally on your device.

B. User-Submitted Content

When you submit a prompt, we collect:

  • Prompt Title — The title of your submission
  • Prompt Content — The full text of the prompt
  • Category — Which category you selected
  • Submitter Name (optional) — Only if you choose to provide it
  • Submission Timestamp — When you submitted it

C. Interaction Data

  • Votes — Which prompts you voted as "Useful" or "Not for me"
  • Copies — Which prompts you copied to clipboard
  • This data helps us rank prompts by popularity and usefulness.

D. Technical Information

  • App version
  • Device type and OS version
  • Crash reports (if enabled via environment configuration)

E. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your real name, email, or contact information (unless voluntarily submitted)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Other app usage
  • ❌ Biometric data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • Device ID — Prevent spam submissions, track votes/copies, rate-limiting
  • Submitted Prompts — Display in the library after moderation approval
  • Votes & Copies — Rank prompts by quality and popularity
  • Optional Name — Credit you as the prompt author (if approved)
  • Crash Reports — Improve app stability and fix bugs

3. Data Retention

  • Device ID: Stored on-device until you uninstall
  • Submitted Prompts: Kept as part of the library indefinitely
  • Votes & Copies: Kept for ranking
  • Anonymous Name: Deleted if submission is rejected

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your information is only used within Prompt Lib.

Exceptions:

  • Moderation team reviews submissions to approve/reject
  • Technical team may view crash reports to fix bugs
  • Legally required disclosures if mandated by law

5. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Device ID is generated and stored locally on your device
  • Server data is protected with standard security measures
  • Submissions are reviewed before publication

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request what data we have about you
  • Delete — Request deletion of your submissions or device data
  • Opt-Out — Disable crash reporting or stop using the app

To exercise these rights, contact: madebybala@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Prompt Lib is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be effective when posted on this page. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices: Email: madebybala@gmail.com

Summary: We collect an anonymous device ID, prompts you submit, and interaction data (votes/copies). We do not collect personal contact info unless you submit it. Data is used to rank prompts, prevent spam, and improve the app.
