App Win Your Week

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

TL;DR — All your habit data stays on your device. Backups are plain files you create and control. The one-time "Plus" purchase is handled by Google Play. We run no servers and collect nothing about you.

1. Overview

Win Your Week ("the App") is a habit and weekly goal tracker designed to help you build consistency. Your data is private by design — there is no account and no backend. All habit records, completions, and progress are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server we operate.

2. Information We Collect

A. On-Device Data (never leaves your device)

  • Habits you create — names, frequencies, schedules, and icons
  • Daily and weekly completion records, streaks, and progress-ring history
  • An optional profile — a display name and a profile photo you choose (saved locally; never uploaded)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Your "Plus" unlock status (see Section 3)
  • Data shown on the optional home-screen widget, drawn from your on-device data

B. Backup & Restore (you control)

You can export your data to a backup file and save or share it anywhere using your device's standard share sheet, and you can restore by selecting a backup file you previously saved. These files are created and controlled entirely by you — we do not receive, store, or have access to them.

C. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, or personal contact information sent to us
  • No account or sign-in
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No biometric data

3. Purchases — "Plus"

Win Your Week offers a one-time "Plus" unlock. Payment is processed entirely by Google Play Billing; we never see or store your payment card details. Google shares with the App only the purchase/entitlement status needed to unlock features. Purchase records are retained by Google Play under its own policies. See the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The App has no backend of its own. It interacts only with the following Google services:

Service Purpose
Google Play BillingProcess the one-time "Plus" purchase
Google Play In-App ReviewShow the optional in-store rating prompt
Google FontsFetch font files at runtime (involves your device's IP address)
Google Play StoreApp distribution and updates

The App does not use any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or server backend.

5. App Permissions

Permission Reason
InternetProcess the in-app purchase and in-store review, and load fonts
NotificationsShow habit and weekly-recap reminders you enable
Run after rebootRe-schedule your local reminders after the device restarts
BillingRequired by Google Play for the one-time "Plus" purchase
Photos (via system picker)Only when you choose a profile photo — saved locally

6. Data Security

  • Your habit data is stored locally on your device and protected by standard app sandboxing
  • Billing and review traffic with Google Play is encrypted by the store
  • We operate no servers that store your data
  • Deleting the App removes all local data from your device

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. Your habit data never leaves your device except in a backup file you explicitly create and control. The only external interactions are with Google Play (purchase and review) and Google Fonts, as described above.

8. Your Rights & Controls

  • Delete your data — Use "Reset All Data" in App Settings, or uninstall the App
  • Export your data — Create a backup file at any time from App Settings
  • Purchase queries — Manage or query purchases through your Google Play account

Because we hold no personal data on any server, there is nothing for us to access or delete on our side. Where laws such as the GDPR or CPRA apply, these rights are satisfied through this on-device control. Questions? Contact madebybala@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Win Your Week is suitable for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Since no personal data is collected or transmitted by us, the App does not pose data-collection privacy risks to younger users.

10. Policy Updates

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
